About Brampton Warriors

Established in 2026, Brampton Warriors is a competitive cricket team built on passion, discipline, and a relentless drive for excellence. Representing the spirit of modern cricket, the team brings together talented players with diverse skill sets, united by a single goal — to dominate the game with fearless intent.

From high-intensity T20 battles to strategic longer formats, Brampton Warriors continues to grow as a strong force in local leagues, showcasing commitment, teamwork, and sportsmanship at every level.
